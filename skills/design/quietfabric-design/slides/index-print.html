Architecture · trade-offs
Every choice bought something, and cost something
what we gained
  • Rail-optimized placement cut all-reduce tail latency.
  • Paged kv-cache raised effective batch size.
  • ECN keeps queues shallow under load.
what it cost
  • Less scheduler freedom under fragmentation.
  • Indirection added on the hot path.
  • Occasional throughput dips during bursts.

Reliability · failure modes
The risk isn't a crash — it's silent backpressure
  • Silent backpressure — pool saturates, queue climbs, latency degrades while liveness still reads healthy.
  • kv-cache exhaustion — occupancy crosses 85%, evictions cascade onto the hot path.
  • Rail fragmentation — scheduler can't co-locate tensor-parallel ranks, collectives slow.
mitigation
Alert on the slope, not the threshold.

By the time a threshold trips, the route has been degrading for minutes. We now page on rate-of-change of queue depth and TTFT.
