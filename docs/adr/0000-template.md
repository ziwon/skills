# NNNN. Short title of the decision

- **Status**: proposed | accepted | superseded by [NNNN](./NNNN-...md)
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What forces are at play? What makes this decision hard to reverse, surprising, or a real
trade-off? (If none of those three apply, you probably don't need an ADR.)

## Decision

What did we decide, stated in the active voice. "We will ..."

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include the alternatives that
were genuinely considered and why they lost.
